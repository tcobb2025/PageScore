What we check

Six factors that make or break your Google ranking

Page Speed Score

How fast your site loads on mobile. Slow sites lose over half their visitors before the page finishes loading.

Meta Descriptions

The text Google shows in search results. Missing or duplicated descriptions mean Google is guessing for you.

Header Tags

H1 and heading structure tell search engines what your page is about. Missing headers hurt your relevance.

Image Optimization

Images without alt text are invisible to Google. We check every image and flag the ones holding you back.

Mobile Friendliness

Over 60% of searches happen on phones. If your site isn't mobile-optimized, you're invisible to most customers.

HTTPS Security

Sites without HTTPS show a "Not Secure" warning. Google penalizes insecure sites and visitors don't trust them.
